This folder is for storing your thesis figures and images.

Supported formats:
- PNG (.png)
- JPEG (.jpg, .jpeg)
- PDF (.pdf)
- EPS (.eps)

Best practices:
1. Use descriptive filenames (e.g., "methodology_flowchart.png" instead of "fig1.png")
2. Keep original high-resolution versions of your figures
3. For vector graphics, prefer PDF or EPS formats
4. For photographs or complex images, use PNG or high-quality JPEG
5. Organize figures in subfolders if you have many (e.g., figures/Chapter1/, figures/Chapter2/)

Example usage in LaTeX:
\begin{figure}[h]
    \centering
    \includegraphics[width=0.8\linewidth]{figures/your_figure.png}
    \caption{Your figure caption here}
    \label{fig:your_label}
\end{figure}
